How to Connect MetaMask

expand_more
  1. Download the MetaMask extension for your browser or mobile app.
  2. Create a new wallet and securely save your Secret Recovery Phrase.
  3. Click 'Connect Wallet' in the top right corner of this page.
  4. Approve the connection request in the MetaMask pop-up.

How to get Sepolia ETH

expand_more

Sepolia ETH is test currency used to pay for gas fees on the Sepolia Testnet. It has no real-world value.

  1. Navigate to a supported Sepolia Faucet (e.g., Alchemy or Infura).
  2. Copy your wallet address from MetaMask (starts with 0x...).
  3. Paste your address into the faucet input field.
  4. Click 'Send Me ETH' to receive your testnet funds within minutes.

What is an NFT Ticket?

expand_more

An NFT Ticket is a unique digital asset stored on the blockchain that proves your ownership of a seat at the event. Unlike paper tickets, it cannot be forged, and it doubles as a digital collectible after the show!
